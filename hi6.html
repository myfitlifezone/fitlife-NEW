How to Prepare: Ginger Tea

  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger, sliced
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  1. Boil water with ginger slices for 10 minutes.
  2. Strain into a cup.
  3. Add honey and lemon juice. Stir well.
  4. Serve warm.

How to Use It:

  • Steep a few slices of fresh ginger in hot water for 5-10 minutes.
  • Drink this tea before or after meals to aid digestion.
