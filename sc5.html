Face masks are a wonderful way to give your skin an extra boost of nourishment. These 5 natural face masks, made with easy-to-find ingredients, can help brighten, hydrate, and rejuvenate your skin, leaving you with a glowing complexion.

Turmeric & Honey Face Mask üçØ

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp yogurt (optional)
How to Make:
  1. Mix turmeric powder, honey, and yogurt (if using) into a smooth paste.
  2. Apply the mask to your face and leave it on for 15-20 minutes, then rinse off with warm water.

Did you know? Turmeric has natural anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that can help brighten the skin and reduce redness, while honey locks in moisture for smooth, glowing skin.

Banana & Avocado Face Mask üçåü•ë

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 ripe banana
  • 1/4 ripe avocado
How to Make:
  1. Mash the banana and avocado together until smooth.
  2. Apply the mixture to your face and leave it on for 15-20 minutes before rinsing off with lukewarm water.

Did you know? Bananas are rich in vitamins and potassium, which hydrate and nourish the skin, while avocado helps provide healthy fats to keep your skin moisturized.

Clay & Aloe Vera Face Mask üåø

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp green clay (or any natural clay)
  • 1 tbsp aloe vera gel
How to Make:
  1. Mix green clay and aloe vera gel to form a paste.
  2. Apply the mask to your face and let it sit for 10-15 minutes before washing off with warm water.

Did you know? Clay helps absorb impurities and excess oil, while aloe vera soothes and hydrates the skin, making this mask great for oily and combination skin.

Oatmeal & Yogurt Face Mask üç∂

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp oatmeal (ground)
  • 2 tbsp plain yogurt
How to Make:
  1. Mix oatmeal and yogurt to form a paste.
  2. Apply the mask to your face and leave it on for 15-20 minutes, then gently rinse off with warm water.

Did you know? Oatmeal helps exfoliate and soothe irritated skin, while yogurt's lactic acid brightens and nourishes the skin, leaving it soft and refreshed.

Cucumber & Yogurt Face Mask ü•í

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 2 tbsp plain yogurt
How to Make:
  1. Blend cucumber into a smooth paste and mix with yogurt.
  2. Apply the mixture to your face and leave it on for 15 minutes before rinsing off with cool water.

Did you know? Cucumber cools and soothes the skin, while yogurt provides hydration and helps balance your skin's natural oils.
