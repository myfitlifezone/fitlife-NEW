Probiotics (Yogurt or Supplements)

Probiotics support gut health by restoring the natural balance of bacteria in the intestines, improving digestion, and boosting overall immunity.

Did you know? Probiotics are beneficial bacteria that help balance the gut microbiome, improving digestion and reducing symptoms like bloating, constipation, and diarrhea.

How to Use: Probiotics

Aloe Vera Juice

Aloe vera juice helps soothe the digestive tract, reduce inflammation, and improve bowel regularity, making it a great remedy for constipation and indigestion.

Did you know? Aloe vera contains enzymes that help break down fats and sugars, reducing inflammation in the digestive system and promoting healthy bowel movements.

How to Use: Aloe Vera Juice

Fiber-Rich Foods

A high-fiber diet promotes regular bowel movements, reduces constipation, and supports overall gut health by keeping your digestive system running smoothly.

Did you know? Fiber is essential for healthy digestion. It adds bulk to stools, prevents constipation, and supports the growth of beneficial gut bacteria.

How to Use: Fiber-Rich Foods

  • Add fiber-rich foods like chia seeds, oats, and beans to your meals.
  • Aim for at least 25-30 grams of fiber per day to support digestion.
