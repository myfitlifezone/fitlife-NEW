A good toner can balance your skin's pH levels, remove any leftover impurities, and prepare your skin for moisturizing or other skincare treatments. These 5 natural face toners are simple to make and work wonders in giving your skin a fresh, rejuvenated look.

Rosewater Toner üåπ

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp rosewater
  • 1/2 cup distilled water
How to Make:
  • Mix rosewater with distilled water in a small bottle or bowl.
  • Apply to your face using a cotton pad after cleansing.

Did you know? Rosewater has natural antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties, which help soothe the skin and reduce redness, giving you a fresh, calm complexion.

Green Tea Toner üçµ

Ingredients:

  • 1 green tea bag
  • 1 cup hot water
How to Make:
  • Brew green tea and let it cool to room temperature.
  • Use a cotton pad to apply the cooled tea to your face.

Did you know? Green tea is packed with antioxidants that help protect the skin from damage and reduce inflammation, making it perfect for a refreshing toner.

Apple Cider Vinegar Toner üçè

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp organic apple cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
How to Make:
  • Dilute apple cider vinegar with water in a small container.
  • Apply to your skin using a cotton ball.

Did you know? Apple cider vinegar helps balance your skin's pH levels, while its natural astringent properties tighten pores and reduce excess oil.

Witch Hazel Toner üåø

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp witch hazel
  • 1/2 cup water
How to Make:
  • Mix witch hazel with water in a bottle.
  • Apply to your face with a cotton pad after cleansing.

Did you know? Witch hazel is a natural astringent that helps reduce inflammation, tightens pores, and removes excess oil, making it ideal for oily or acne-prone skin.

Cucumber Toner ü•í

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1/4 cup distilled water
How to Make:
  • Blend cucumber until smooth and strain the juice.
  • Mix the cucumber juice with distilled water and apply to your face using a cotton pad.

Did you know? Cucumber has hydrating and soothing properties, which help refresh the skin, reduce puffiness, and improve skin texture.
