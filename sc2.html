Exfoliating your skin is an essential part of a skincare routine to remove dead skin cells, promote circulation, and reveal a fresh, glowing complexion. These 5 natural face scrubs use simple ingredients that nourish your skin while gently exfoliating, leaving you with smooth, radiant skin.

Brown Sugar & Honey Scrub üçØ

A natural exfoliant with brown sugar and honey, this scrub gently sloughs off dead skin cells while hydrating and soothing your skin.

Did you know? Brown sugar is a natural exfoliant that gently sloughs off dead skin cells, while honey hydrates and helps soothe the skin.

How to Prepare: Brown Sugar & Honey Scrub

  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  1. Mix brown sugar and honey to form a paste.
  2. Gently massage it onto your face in circular motions for about 2-3 minutes, then rinse with warm water.

This scrub fights aging with antioxidants from coffee and deeply moisturizes with coconut oil, preventing skin dryness.

Did you know? Coffee contains antioxidants that help fight aging, while coconut oil provides deep moisture and prevents skin dryness.

How to Prepare: Coffee & Coconut Oil Scrub

  • 1 tbsp ground coffee
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  1. Mix ground coffee and coconut oil to create a thick paste.
  2. Apply the scrub to your face and gently massage for 3-5 minutes before rinsing with warm water.

This scrub nourishes your skin with almonds rich in vitamin E while honey locks in moisture for a natural glow.

Did you know? Almonds are rich in vitamin E, which nourishes the skin, while honey locks in moisture and provides a natural glow.

  • 2 tbsp ground almonds
  • 1 tbsp honey

  2. Gently massage it onto your face for 3 minutes, then rinse off with warm water.
