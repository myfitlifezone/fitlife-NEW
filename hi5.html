Rosemary Oil üåø

Rosemary oil is a powerful remedy for thinning hair. It improves blood flow to the scalp, strengthens hair follicles, and helps prevent hair loss.

Did you know? Rosemary oil has been shown to increase circulation to the scalp, stimulate hair follicles, and promote hair growth by blocking the effects of DHT (a hormone linked to hair loss).

How to Use: Rosemary Oil

  • 5-6 drops of rosemary essential oil
  • 1 tbsp carrier oil (coconut or olive oil)
  1. Mix rosemary oil with the carrier oil.
  2. Massage onto the scalp for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Leave it on for 30-60 minutes, then wash with shampoo.

Aloe vera is a natural conditioner that strengthens hair, reduces dandruff, and promotes a healthy scalp environment.

Did you know? Aloe vera contains proteolytic enzymes that repair dead skin cells on the scalp and promote healthy hair growth.

How to Use: Aloe Vera for Hair

  • Fresh aloe vera gel
  1. Extract fresh aloe vera gel from the plant.
  2. Massage directly into the scalp and leave for 30-45 minutes.
  3. Rinse off thoroughly.

Fenugreek Seeds üå±

Fenugreek seeds are a powerful natural remedy for hair growth. They help prevent hair thinning, nourish the scalp, and encourage new hair growth.

How to Use: Fenugreek Seeds
